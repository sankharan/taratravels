Sri Lanka Mini Tour - 05 Nights

Colombo, Sigiriya, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Bentota
Private Tour
From $599 per person
Book This Tour

Tour Overview

Experience the best of Sri Lanka in this compact 6-day tour that takes you through ancient cities, lush highlands, and pristine beaches. This mini tour package is perfect for travelers with limited time who want to experience the diversity of Sri Lanka's landscapes and culture.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya
  • Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy
  • Experience the cool climate of Nuwara Eliya's tea country
  • Enjoy beach relaxation in Bentota
  • Discover Colombo's vibrant city life
  • Traditional cultural dance show in Kandy

Detailed Itinerary

1

Airport Pickup - Colombo City Tour

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel in Colombo. After freshening up, embark on a city tour of Colombo, visiting key attractions including Gangaramaya Temple, Independence Square, and the bustling Pettah Market. Overnight stay in Colombo.

2

Colombo - Sigiriya

After breakfast, depart for Sigiriya. En route, visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site featuring stunning Buddhist murals and statues. In the afternoon, climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an ancient palace and fortress complex with remarkable water gardens, frescoes, and the famous Lion's Gate. Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

3

Sigiriya - Kandy

After breakfast, travel to Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka. En route, visit a spice garden in Matale to learn about Sri Lanka's renowned spices and their medicinal properties. In Kandy, visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of Buddhism's most sacred sites. In the evening, enjoy a traditional cultural dance performance. Overnight stay in Kandy.

4

Kandy - Nuwara Eliya - Bentota

After breakfast, travel to Nuwara Eliya, often called "Little England" due to its colonial-era bungalows and cool climate. Visit a tea plantation and factory to learn about Ceylon tea production. Enjoy the scenic landscapes before continuing to Bentota. Overnight stay in Bentota.

5

Bentota Beach Day

Enjoy a full day at leisure in Bentota. Relax on the golden sandy beaches, swim in the Indian Ocean, or engage in water sports (optional at extra cost). You may also take an optional boat safari on the Madu River to explore the mangrove forests and visit a turtle hatchery. Overnight stay in Bentota.

6

Bentota - Airport Departure

After breakfast, depending on your flight schedule, you'll be transferred to the airport for your departure flight, marking the end of your Sri Lanka mini tour.
