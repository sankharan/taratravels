Discover Bentota

Nestled along Sri Lanka's southwestern coast, Bentota is a tropical paradise renowned for its pristine golden beaches, tranquil lagoon, and lush coconut palm groves. This coastal town offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Bentota's unique geography, where the Bentota River meets the Indian Ocean, creates a stunning landscape of waterways, mangroves, and beaches that have made it one of Sri Lanka's most popular tourist destinations.

Things to Do in Bentota

Experience the best of beach life and adventure

Water Sports

Experience thrilling water activities including jet skiing, banana boat rides, windsurfing, and more in Bentota's calm waters.

River Safari

Explore the Bentota River on a boat safari, discovering mangroves, exotic birds, and traditional fishing villages.

Beach Relaxation

Unwind on Bentota's pristine beaches, enjoy spectacular sunsets, and experience true tropical bliss.

Turtle Sanctuary Visit

Visit the Kosgoda Turtle Sanctuary to learn about conservation efforts and see endangered sea turtles up close.
