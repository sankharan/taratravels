Tour Overview

Experience the best of Sri Lanka in this compact 5-night/6-day tour that takes you through ancient cities, lush highlands, and pristine beaches. This mini tour package is perfect for travelers with limited time who want to experience the diversity of Sri Lanka's landscapes and culture.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya
  • Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy
  • Experience the cool climate of Nuwara Eliya's tea country
  • Enjoy beach relaxation in Bentota
  • Discover Colombo's vibrant city life
  • Traditional cultural dance show in Kandy
  • Spice garden visit in Matale
  • Scenic train ride through tea plantations

Detailed Itinerary

1

Arrival in Colombo

Transfer: Airport to Colombo (1 hour)
Activity Level: Easy

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel in Colombo. After freshening up, embark on a city tour of Colombo, visiting key attractions including Gangaramaya Temple, Independence Square, and the bustling Pettah Market. Overnight stay in Colombo.

2

Colombo - Dambulla - Sigiriya

Transfer: Colombo to Dambulla (3.5 hours), Dambulla to Sigiriya (30 mins)
Activity Level: Moderate (Sigiriya climb)
Entrance Fees: Dambulla Cave Temple - $10, Sigiriya - $30

After breakfast, depart for Dambulla. Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site featuring stunning Buddhist murals and statues. In the afternoon, climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an ancient palace and fortress complex with remarkable water gardens, frescoes, and the famous Lion's Gate. Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

3

Sigiriya - Matale - Kandy

Transfer: Sigiriya to Matale (1.5 hours), Matale to Kandy (1.5 hours)
Activity Level: Moderate
Entrance Fees: Temple of the Tooth - $10, Cultural Show - $10

After breakfast, depart for Kandy. En route, visit a spice garden in Matale to see various spices and learn about their medicinal uses. Arrive in Kandy and visit the Temple of the Tooth, which houses the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha. In the evening, enjoy a traditional cultural dance show. Overnight stay in Kandy.

4

Kandy - Nuwara Eliya

Transfer: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya (3 hours)
Activity Level: Easy
Entrance Fees: Tea Factory visit - $5

After breakfast, depart for Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England" due to its colonial architecture and cool climate. En route, visit a tea plantation and factory to learn about the tea-making process and enjoy a fresh cup of Ceylon tea. In Nuwara Eliya, visit Gregory Lake, Victoria Park, and the post office building. Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

5

Nuwara Eliya - Bentota

Transfer: Nuwara Eliya to Bentota (5-6 hours)
Activity Level: Easy

After breakfast, depart for Bentota, a popular beach destination. En route, enjoy scenic views of the countryside. Upon arrival, check in to your beachfront hotel and spend the rest of the day relaxing on the golden sands or enjoying water sports (optional at extra cost). Overnight stay in Bentota.

6

Bentota - Colombo - Departure

Transfer: Bentota to Colombo (1.5 hours), Colombo to Airport (1 hour)
Activity Level: Easy

After breakfast, enjoy some free time at the beach. Later, transfer to Colombo for some last-minute shopping or sightseeing. In the evening, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, marking the end of your Sri Lanka Mini Tour.
